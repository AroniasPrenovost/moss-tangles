Hand-crafted moss creations

Sustainable and crafty nature tangles for work, home, and zen.

Each unique terrarium is created using a collection of native Pacific Northwest mosses, roots, branches, and pebbles - assembled in harmony among select house plants.

Care Instructions: Simply spray the plants and moss every other day and keep out of direct sunlight. This will maintain a flourishing indoor garden to enjoy.
